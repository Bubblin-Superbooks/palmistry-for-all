Chapter Vii

The Mount Of Jupiter And Its Meaning

The Mount of Jupiter is found at the base of the first finger (., Part II.). When large, it shows desire to dominate, to rule and command others, to lead and organise, and to carry out some distinct object. But these good qualities will only be employed if the Line of Head is clear and long.
