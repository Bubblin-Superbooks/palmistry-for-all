Chapter Vi

The Mount Of Mars

This Mount has two positions on the palm (., Part II.); the first is to be found immediately under the upper part of the Line of Life, and the other opposite to it in the space lying between the Line of Heart and the Line of Head. The first relates to the physical characteristics and the second to the mental.
