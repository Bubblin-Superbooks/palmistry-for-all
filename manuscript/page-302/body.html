Chapter Viii

The Mount Of Saturn And Its Meaning

The Mount of Saturn is found at the base of the second finger (see ., Part II.). Its chief characteristics are love of solitude, prudence, quiet determination, the study of serious sombre things, the belief in fatalism and in the ultimate destiny of all things.
