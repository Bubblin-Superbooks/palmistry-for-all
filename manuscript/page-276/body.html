Mercury

=

Mentality, commerce, science.

Moon

=

Imagination, romance, changeability.

Sun

=

Brilliancy, fruitfulness, success.

Jupiter

=

Ambition, power, domination.

Saturn

=

Reserve, melancholy, seriousness.

In my own long experience I could not help but remark the intimate relation between the effect of these great planets of our Universe and humanity in general.
